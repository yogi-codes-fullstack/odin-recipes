Egg Omelette


Description

Known as the easiest and tastiest quick snack recipe

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat a pan
  2. Put butter/oil into the pan. Let it heat for a couple of minutes
  3. Crack open 2 eggs into the pan, add chilli and salt according to your taste
  4. Let it cook for 2 min, flip over the eggs to cook the other side of it
  5. Serve it hot
