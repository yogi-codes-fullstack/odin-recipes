Butter Chicken


Description

A special cuisine from the Northern State of India - Punjab

Despite being a dish from Indian Subcontinent, its recognised as the national dish of United Kingdom.

Steps to Prepare

  1. Take a kadahi and put some mustard oil
  2. After the oil heats up, add onion, garlic tomato, black pepper, cumin powder and other masala into the kadahi and cook for atleast 20 min
  3. Put chicken pieces into it. Cook for another 20 mins
  4. Put some water according to your requirements, and let it simmer for 5 mins till the water boils
  5. Serve it hot
